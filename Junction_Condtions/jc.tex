\subsection*{Pouring equation to write in the future}

\textcolor{red}{Do not forget to add the D dimensional case to refer to it when corrections to the action are used}

$(R_T)_{abcd}=R^{(4)}_{abcd}$ is the induced curvature on the brane and is related to the bulk curvature $R^{(4+d)}_{\mu \nu \rho \sigma}$ and the extrinsic curvature $K^i _{ab}$, with $i=\left\{1 ... d \right\}$ through the Gauss-Codazzi (GC) equations:
\begin{equation}
R^{(4+d)}_{\mu \nu \rho \sigma} e^\mu_a e^\nu_b e^\rho_c e^\sigma_d = (R_T)_{abcd} + \delta_{ij}\left(K^i _{ad} K^j _{bc} - K^i _{ac} K^j _{bd}\right),
\end{equation}
where $e^\mu_a$ projects the Riemann tensor of the bulk onto the brane.
